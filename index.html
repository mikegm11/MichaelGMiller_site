About

I’m currently a dual Masters degree candidate at Northwestern University’s Kellogg School of Management, where I will receive an MBA and MS in Design Innovation (McCormick School of Engineering). In this dual-degree program (Kellogg’s MMM program – Multidisciplinary, MS, MBA) we focus on integrating business with empathy-driven design, technical expertise, and human-centered innovation.

Before Kellogg, I worked at EY working with companies to operate effectively in a global economy. During my time at the firm, I worked on digital products for our service line and clients to digitize and streamline steps of the compliance process. While developing a passion for elegant digital solutions, I learned to effectively manage products and cross-functional teams, coach and mentor staff, and build complex data models. My time at EY influenced me greatly in that I learned how to root digital solutions for businesses in people and processes.

I hope to bring my cross-functional skillset to a role facilitating cooperative innovation among engineering, design, product, marketing, finance, and other business functions. I'm always ready to get my hands dirty and jump into the weeds of a problem.
